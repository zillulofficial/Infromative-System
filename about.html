CONCERNS ON LOCATING DOCTORS

There are qualified physicians in our country, but the problem is that we don't know precisely where they are. We frequently don't know which hospital or doctor would be most appropriate for our needs. Due of this issue, patients often undergo inadequate medical care.

OUR ATTEMPTS TO FIX THE PROBLEM

By giving all the hospitals in a certain region's information, we offer medical help. We also display medical professionals who are experts in the listed disorders. Thus, whenever someone searches for an illness, they will get all the physicians who are specialized in that disease, along with information like where they can be found.

MEDIOCRE RESTAURANTS

We enjoy spending time with our loved ones at restaurants, but the problem is that most frequently we go into a restaurant without knowing about its reputation or the standard of its food. Therefore, we place an unsure order for food, which we regret afterwards.

GOOD FOOD, GOOD TIMES

Our website will automatically display restaurants that serve a specific meal when a user types in the name of that food, like "burger," for example. Additionally, details like ratings, reviews, and items will be displayed. Furthermore, anyone searching for information about a restaurant will be able to find it by simply typing the name.
